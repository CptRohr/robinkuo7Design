BRAND IDENTITY PROJECT

A comprehensive brand identity system for a modern tech startup

CLIENT Tech Startup Inc.
DATE December 2024
ROLE Lead Designer
CATEGORY Branding

PROJECT OVERVIEW

This project involved creating a complete brand identity system for a technology startup looking to make waves in the digital transformation space. The challenge was to create a brand that felt both cutting-edge and approachable, professional yet innovative.

The solution combines bold geometric shapes with a vibrant color palette, creating a visual language that speaks to innovation while maintaining accessibility and clarity.

MAIN PROJECT IMAGE

THE CHALLENGE

Creating a brand that stands out in a crowded tech market while maintaining professional credibility. The client needed an identity that could scale across digital and print media, appealing to both B2B and B2C audiences.

THE SOLUTION

A flexible brand system built on strong geometric foundations with a bold color palette. The identity includes a custom logomark, typography system, and comprehensive brand guidelines ensuring consistency across all touchpoints.
